# Social Graph

**One job:** Who do I know who can get me to this person?

Not a CRM. A small relationship map for warm intros.

## Screens

1. **Network** — see people you know, then who they know
2. **Find** — pick or search a target → see who to ask for an intro
3. **Rate** — import contacts, score relationships (1–10), confirm with a swipe
4. **Note** — open a person; mark warmth / awkward intros

## Automatic vs manual

| Automatic | Manual |
|-----------|--------|
| Path search + ranking from your graph | Who you actually know (saved locally / synced) |
| Link strength from evidence on edges | Private notes |
| Optional AI scoring for imported contacts | “This intro is awkward” judgment |

**Honest scope:** Your contacts + optional sample map ship with the app. There is no live public-web crawl yet — the sample NYC / Trump path is illustrative seed data, not verified private access.

## Look & feel

- **Like Obsidian:** graph, notes, clickable links, quiet UI
- **A little like a CRM:** tags / “I know them” only as side metadata
- **Never:** spreadsheets, pipelines, uncited AI claims, dashboard clutter
